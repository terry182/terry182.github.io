About

Greetings, I am Hou Teng Cheong, currently a M.S. student in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from NTU in 2018.

My research interest includes algorithm design, computational complexity and graph theory. Recently my research focus on hole detecting algorithms.

I am going to receive my M.S. degree in this year and looking for a Ph.D. position in Fall 2021.
